Rooms

Three rooms with a chic style, wait at the top of a beautiful double staircase, vintage William Morris wallpapers, bold colors, pretty fire places and the odd armoire. The rooms are ideal for those wanting to dine or simple savor the charms of Laugharne, don’t miss Laugharne for all things Dylan Thomas.

All three bedrooms have been totally refurbished (2016) and all have on suite facilities, they also include flat screen TV’s, tea making facilities, Nespresso machines and free WIFI.

The Blue Room

A garden facing double room on the first floor, lovely views of the garden through the Victorian shutter window. The furniture is all stained pitched pine with a king size bed

The Jade Room

A garden facing double room on the first floor, lovely views of the garden through the Victorian shutter window. The double bed is an antique metal and brass with a free standing armoire in the room.

The Gold Room

The larger of the three rooms is the Gold Room, with a double bed and spacious seating options. The bathroom is huge, with a corner bath and free standing shower, super soft towels, bath robes and luxury toiletry's. The décor is modern with lots of metallic wallcoverings and contemporary artworks.
