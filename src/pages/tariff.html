Dining

The Cors Country House can cater for small functions, business functions, parties and birthdays. The minimum amount of guests to book an exclusive dining experience is 10 + (35 maximum).

The food will feature old and new favorites from the original restaurant menu, classic examples include local salt marsh lamb, smoked haddock crème brulle and plenty of local fish and sea food.

The menu is a la carte with a choice of 5 starters and 5 main courses with a section of puddings. The price point will be around £30-35 for three courses pending on supplements.

We are fully licensed and have an extensive wine list.

Please call for specific menu and pricing options.
