Private Dining

 After nearly 30 years in the restaurant business the time has come to move away from ‘fyne dining’ to a more relaxed style. The main emphasis will be on the accommodation, with more of a brasserie/bistro concept for the food.

 For me, of course the food must be good; however, it’s the whole package, atmosphere and ambiance that count.

The Cors Country House caters for small functions, business functions, parties and birthdays. The minimum number of guests to an exclusive dining experience is 10 + (35 maximum). 

The Brasserie food will feature old and new favourites from the original Cors restaurant menu: classic examples include local salt marsh lamb, smoked haddock crème brûlée, plus plenty of local fish and sea food. 

The menu for private dining will still be à la carte with a choice of 5 starters, 5 main courses and a selection of puddings. The price range will be between £30-35 for three courses pending on supplements.

Brasserie/Bistro food will be available most weekends. Please check for opening times.

 We are fully licensed and have an extensive wine list.
