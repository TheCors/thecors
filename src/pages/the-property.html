The Property

About The Cors Country House

Come and experience the bohemian bolthole as an entire house tucked away in the middle of this attractive little town dominated by a ruined castle overlooking the estuary is this rather unexpected country house with rooms. Set in two and a half acres of formally landscaped gardens and with an elegant facade, it is every inch the country villa from the outside, so the interior, with its air of French grandeur, comes as something as a revelation. The exotic garden is a beautiful retreat in the summer. Gunnera, bamboo and tree ferns flourish while the river Corran potters past – perfect for summer gatherings!

A delightful kitchen is ideally equipped for rustling up a feast for your loved ones, and why not head outside to dine alfresco. Alternatively in the cooler months, serve up your meals in the French style romantic dining room with beautiful views through to the sun room and beyond.

The quirky bar and games room with its French doors onto the veranda, offers a variety of board games, or why not shoot some pool, or shake up some cocktails behind the bar. For some chill out time why not relax on the large corner sofa in the cinema room and watch a movie on the 60 inch television.

A good night’s sleep is guaranteed in this tranquil haven, with the first floor accommodating 3 large double ensuite bedrooms, with a further bathroom with bathtub and additional sleeping for an extra two persons in the loft room on a sofa bed.

The exotic garden is a beautiful retreat in the summer. Gunnera, bamboo and tree ferns flourish while the river Corran potters past – perfect for summer gatherings! It is an exceptional, elegant and artistic garden with unusual architectural and exotic planting including sculptures which will be sure to impress any guest.
